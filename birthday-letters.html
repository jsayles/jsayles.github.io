Write Me a Letter

I'm turning 50 and I'd like to make a request. If you're here, you're someone whose perspective I trust.

What I'm asking for is a letter, but what I'm really asking for is to be seen by you and to see what you see in me.

Handwritten, typed, short, long, whatever form feels right.

Some prompts if you need help getting started:

  • What in me has aged well?
  • What have you learned from our time together?
  • Tell me the story of someone in your life who inspired you as they aged.
  • A memory of us you think about sometimes.
  • Something you hope for me in the years ahead.

How to get it to me

  • Email it to birthday@jacobsayles.com
  • Hand it to me on paper
  • Text it, mail it, whatever works

You don't have to answer all the prompts. You don't have to answer any of them. I just want to hear from you in your own way.
