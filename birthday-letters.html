Write Me a Birthday Letter

Hey — if you're here, I asked you personally. Thank you.

For my birthday this year, I'd love a letter from you. Handwritten, typed, short, long, funny, serious. Whatever feels right.

A few loose parameters

  • Tell me something you've never told me
  • Or a memory of us you think about sometimes
  • Or what you hope for me in the year ahead
  • Or just say hi in a way that's really you

How to get it to me

  • Email it to birthday@jacobsayles.com
  • Hand it to me on paper
  • Text it, mail it, whatever works

No pressure on length or polish. I just want to hear from you.
